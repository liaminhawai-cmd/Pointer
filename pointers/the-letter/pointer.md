# The Letter

Imagine you are reading a letter and then you're going to respond to it. It feels like you have free will to make choices about what you do.

As you're typing, your fingers are moving and clicking the keys. Do they have free will? The muscles contracting to make your fingers move, do they have free will?

There are definitely electrical signals. There are definitely muscles moving. There are definitely thoughts appearing in your mind before you type.

But did you think the thoughts before you thought them? Did you choose the thoughts before you thought them? And if you did, did you choose to choose them?

If there's no chooser anywhere in this, it's no different from dropping a ball and watching it bounce. Just causes and effects.
