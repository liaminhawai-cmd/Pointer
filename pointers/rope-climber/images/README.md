# Rope Climber artwork

Panels in reading order:

- `01-climb.jpg`       — the ordinary climb. The lead image; no hint of rope-body yet.
- `02-foot.jpg`        — close on the shoe: the foot is woven rope, fused to the rope below.
- `03-knot-body.jpg`   — pull back: the whole body is knotted rope hauling on itself.
- `04-eye-knot.jpg`    — the body resolves into an eye made of coiled rope.
- `05-empty-cliff.jpg` — the cliff with just the rope hanging. No climber.

To change the order or the ending, reorder the `panels` array in `../index.html`.

Keep the reveal: never use `03`/`04` as a thumbnail or social preview. The first
thing seen must be the ordinary climb.

Note: `02-foot.jpg` originally had a "Posted in r/nonduality" banner across the
bottom; it has been cropped off.
