# Rope Climber artwork

Drop the panel images in here, named in reading order:

- `01-climb.png`     — the ordinary climb (wide + small panels). The lead image. No hint of rope-body yet.
- `02-foot.png`      — close on the shoe/ankle: the foot is woven rope, fused to the rope below.
- `03-knot-body.png` — pull back: the whole body is knotted rope hauling on itself.
- `04-eye-knot.png`  — the body resolves into an eye made of coiled rope.
- `05-empty-cliff.png` — the cliff with just the rope hanging. No climber.

`.png` or `.jpg` are both fine — just update the filenames in `../index.html` if you change the extension.

When the files are in, flip `"published": true` for `rope-climber` in `/pointers.json` and it appears on the index.

Keep the reveal: never use `03`/`04` as a thumbnail or social preview. The first thing seen must be the ordinary climb.
