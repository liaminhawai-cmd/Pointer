# The Puppet and the Sheet

A cloth puppet walks around on a set made of soft fabric. It looks down and sees that its feet are part of the fabric.

So it starts to unravel, back into the set it was standing on. As it comes undone, the fabric it was bunching pulls flat.

It sinks back to just a head, then just an eye watching the world, then the eye unravels too.

And there is just a flat sheet.
